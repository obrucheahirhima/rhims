RHIMS was founded on a single observation: most founders in Africa are building alone. The vision is there. The drive is real. But somewhere between the idea and the execution, momentum stalls — because there's no one in the room who can hold the brand and the business at the same time.

We exist to be that person. RHIMS is a full-service brand strategy, creative, and operations partner for consumer brands in beauty, fashion, lifestyle, and FMCG.
